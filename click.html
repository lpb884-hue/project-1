Why I Like It

It is probably suprising that there is a movie on this list after Happy Gilmore because of how much I was singing that movies praises. Coming in at #1, my favorite Adam Sandler movie, Click. Click is such an underated Adam Sandler movie, and I think that's part of the reason I like it so much. This movie is a take on the term "living life on autopilot". Adam Sandler's character get a magic remote and starts fast forwarding through life until its too late. This is an emotional movie towards the end as the effects of the remote start to take a toll on his life. This is the first movie that I can remember watching as a kid that really made me cry. This is a beautiful movie about cherishing the fleeting moments of everyday life and I will forever recommend it to anyone who hasn't seen it.
