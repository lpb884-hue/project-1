#6: 50 First Dates
(2004)

Cast Top 3 (Besides Adam Sandler)

Drew Barrymore

Rob Schneider

Sean Astin

Why I Like It

At #6, I have what I think is Adam Sandler's best true rom-coms, and also one of the best rom-coms of all time. The plot of this movie is very unique and cool, and I enjoy the Hawaii setting of it. This movie created a fictional version of amnesia, where Drew Barrymore's character experiences the same day over and over again because of a traumatic head injury. Adam Sandler's character having to win her over everyday makes for a very interesting love story. We also get to see how Drew Barrymore's dad and brother have to take care of her, and it is powerful to see the lengths they go to not have her find out about her accident.
