#9: The Waterboy (1998)

Cast Top 3 (Besides Adam Sandler)

Henry Winkler

Kathy Bates

Fairuza Balk

Why I Like It

The Waterboy comes in at #9 on my list. I think that the Waterboy is one of the best sports comedies of all time. This movie, like most Sandler movies, is a good time, and a fun movie. The Waterboy is one of those Adam Sandler movies that is viewed as a classic, and it is right up there with his other pre-2000s movies. This movie has very interesting, wacky characters, and the Louisiana bayou is a fun setting for this story. I really enjoy sports movies, especially football, so I am a big fan of this movie and Sandler's other football movie "The Longest Yard".
