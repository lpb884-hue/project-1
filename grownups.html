Why I Like It

Grown Ups is far and away Sandler's best movie with an ensemble cast. I have this movie at #4 for basically that exact reason. Adam Sandler gets all of his buddies together in this one, and it is a blast. I would say that this movie is Sandler's most modern classic, as it is one of his more well-known movies post-2000s. This movie has so many characters, but none of them are forgettable. Grown Ups is a great summer, feel-good, buddy movie and propably my most rewatched Adam Sandler movie besides Happy Gilmore. If this movie is ever on, I will sit down and watch the rest of it because its just that good and rewatchable.
