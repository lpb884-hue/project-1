Why I Like It

At #3, I have Uncut Gems. Uncut Gems is unlike other movies on this list, because it is less of an Adam Sandler movie and more of a movie that happens to have Adam Sandler in it, if that makes sense. This movie is by far the best movie on this list, just not the best Sandler one. In it, Adam Sandler delivers what I think is his best performance ever in a role that proves that he isn't just a one-dimensional comedy actor. Uncut Gems is a fantastic story about the destructive nature of addiction, and there are thrills and suspense throughout the whole thing that is matched by the movies killer soundtrack.
