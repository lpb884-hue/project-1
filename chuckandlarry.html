Why I Like It

At #7, I have another Adam Sandler that is different from the rest. This movie has the most serius themes out of any of his movies, even if it isn't taking itself to seriously. I Now Pronounce You Chuck & Larry is about the dismantling of homophobia and the importance of family. Even though this movie is funny, it has a very good message of being comfortable in sexuality, and being openluy gay in masculine environments such as a firehouse. I also like this movie because Kevin James feels like more of a lead character in this one, and Adam Sandler does well in a backseat kind of role.
