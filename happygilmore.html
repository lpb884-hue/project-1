Why I Like It

THE Adam Sandler movie. Everyone should know this movie. At #2 with strong case for #1, I have Happy Gilmore. I absolutely love this movie. I think that Happy Gilmore is the best sports comedy of all time, and one of the best sports movies in general. It's funny, inspiring, and so rewatchable. I don't think I could ever get tired of this movie. If I was stranded on and island and could only watch one movie it'd probably be this one. Happy Gilmore has such a great love interest in Julie Bowen's character, and Christopher McDonald plays a fantastic villian as Shooter McGavin. The cast of this movie is stacked, the soundtrack is great, and the golf scenes manage to not feel super repetative. Happy Gilmore 2 is a digrace to the legacy of this movie. R.I.P. Chubbs.
